IT Paragraph Builder

Follow the two steps below to build your outline and generate a color-coded paragraph.

Step 1: Build a Body Paragraph Outline

Complete each section below. Expand the instructions if you need help. When you are done, click "Generate Outline" and copy the result into your Google Doc for revision.

Supporting Idea (Topic Sentence) — instructions

This is the main idea your paragraph will prove. This is a single sentence in which you state the idea clearly and avoid mentioning any sources.

Evidence 1 — instructions

Choose the strongest evidence that supports your idea. Try to keep direct quotes below 35 words.

Why it matters — instructions

Explain briefly why this piece of evidence is useful for your paragraph. What does it show?

Evidence 2 — instructions

Choose another strong piece of evidence that supports your idea. It should relate to your first piece in some way.

Why it matters — instructions

Explain briefly why this second piece of evidence is useful. Does it build on the other evidence?

Commentary / Reasoning — instructions

Explain how each piece of evidence helps prove your supporting idea. This step turns information from your sources into your own argument.

Body Paragraph Outline

Step 2: Create Your Body Paragraph

After editing your outline in your Google Doc, paste the entire revised outline into the box below and click "Convert to Paragraph".

Important: For best results, do not change the bolded labels (e.g., "Topic Sentence", "Evidence 1") when editing. The app will try to understand minor changes, but keeping the labels is the safest way to ensure it works correctly.
